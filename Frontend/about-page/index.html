About BruchinDB

BruchinDB is a scientific data platform dedicated to advancing knowledge of seed beetles (Bruchinae) and their ecological and agricultural significance. Originally compiled over decades by Dr. Geoffrey Morse, the database brings together taxonomic records, specimen data, host plant associations, geographic distributions, and images into a single accessible resource. Its mission is to make high-quality entomological information widely available to researchers, educators, conservation professionals, and the public, supporting scientific discovery, biodiversity understanding, and informed agricultural decision-making. Guided by values of accuracy, accessibility, and collaboration, BruchinDB transforms specialized research data into a tool for global learning and environmental insight.
